Are You Smarter Than a Monkey?

This is a trivia game where you play against a virtual monkey. The goal is to reach 10 points faster than the monkey.

The monkey randomly selects answers with a 50% chance of being correct.

Your task is to answer trivia questions correctly to earn points. If you reach 10 points before the monkey, you win!
